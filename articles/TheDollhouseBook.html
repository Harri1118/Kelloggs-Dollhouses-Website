The Doll House Book
by Stephanie Finnegan
(available at Amazon)

The Doll House Book, by Stephanie Finnegan, traces the history of dollhouses with text and photos. Ned's outstanding replica of a Queen Anne Tudor House from Rochester, New York, graces the title page. There is also within the book a chapter on Ned Kellogg with photos of his work and biography. A quote from the book by Editorial Director June Lange of Dollhouse Miniatures Magazine: "He creates houses that are so authentic looking you can't believe they're miniatures".
