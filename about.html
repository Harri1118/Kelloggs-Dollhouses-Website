About us

Since 1977, KELLOGG'S DOLLS' HOUSES had been offering our exclusive hand-built dollhouses by the late Master Craftsman, NED KELLOGG, whose work has been featured on TV, newspapers, magazines and books.

We are continuing the tradition of carrying a line of hand-built basic Capes, Colonials, Victorians and farmhouses starting at $85 for a four-room Cape Cod, all constructed of 3/8" cabinet-grade birch plywood and following Mr. Kellogg's styles. We will be expanding this line and will be adding them to our site. Over the years, Mr. Kellogg passed on much of his expertise and knowledge to his wife, SONIA KELLOGG, who has taken over the business and she is using his techniques and finishing materials as were taught to her by Mr. Kellogg. Since each house is individually hand cut, it is possible to have architectural detailing such as towers, gables, porches, extensions and porticos. Let us make a house for you tailored to your needs. This site is dedicated to NED KELLOGG and you may visit the Archives to view some of his work. His remaining museum-quality houses can also be viewed in our retail shop in Cold Spring Harbor, Long Island, New York.

The pricing list reflects the prices for our basic hand-built houses in an unfinished, but totally built state and there are numerous possibilities which we can offer you. Please feel free to consult with us for your dollhouse needs. We can design a house for you within the price range that best suits you. Any house can be finished off or can be purchased unfinished and we will guide you through the finished process offering our expertise and finishing materials at double discount prices.

Kellogg's can replicate any structure, existing or non-existing in scale. Some of our current replicas can be seen in our Gallery or on Facebook where you can also see numerous older house restorations and remodels.

We specialize in restoring and refurbishing used and older dollhouses. Photos of these restorations can be sent to you upon request should you need this service.

We carry all of the major lines of miniature furniture and accessories at our discounted prices ranging from 10% to 20% off list price and the purchaser of a dollhouse from us is entitled to additional discounts on these products. We will ship whatever is needed. Let us be your full-service dollhouse shop for all of your miniature furniture and accessory and building/finishing materials.

We look forward to serving you and filling your dollhouse needs.
